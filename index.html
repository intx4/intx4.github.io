Hello there! I'm Francesco, 22-year old boy coming from Siracusa, Sicily, a Southern pearl of Italy. I am currently enrolled in a joint MSc. program at EPFL (and ETH). I am one of the many high-school students who were quite good at math, so I decided to pursue a degree in Engineering (originally Management Engineering). During my first year at Politecnico di Torino, when I was 19, I discovered that I really liked programming, so I switched path and became a Computer Engineering student. During my last year, thanks to the CyberChallenge.IT program, I developed quite an interest for topics concerning security and privacy... so here I am now :)
When I am not failing at hacking stuff (mostly during CTFs I play in my free time) or building some super complex systems, I enjoy doing sports or hanging around with friends. Actually, I am super weird also when it comes to picking sports, since I have been playing Canoe Polo since I was 14 (if you don't know what I'm talking about, check this out: Dope Canoe Polo video).
I also quite like cooking (mostly Italian recipes...), playing video games ("Git gud" if you know what I mean), and reading books (especially Camilleri's crime fiction "Inspector Montalbano").
